Exploring Haji Lane!

18 January 2022

During the term break and as part of IAP 2022, SUTDio organised a mural tour along the streets of the Kampong Glam area to discover murals and other forms of street art on Haji Lane. On this relaxingly paced (free) tour, students learnt the meaning behind some of the murals and dabbled in some street photography to capture picturesque creations and frame iconic architectural works.
