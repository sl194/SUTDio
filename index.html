About Us

What we do

Welcome to
SUTDio's website!

SUTDio is an SUTD's very own "archi" club, founded in 2013 by the early batches of SUTD students!

Our club has weekly sessions on Thursday evenings, from 7-10pm, but sometimes it may also be twice a week, from 7-10pm on Tuesdays as well. We conduct myriad workshops, ranging from educational ones to fun ones! The educational workshops involve imparting skills related to design and architecture to students, whereas the fun ones could go from making silkscreen T-shirts to pixel art. On top of that, we also take part in external events and festivals such as PARK(ing) Day and i Light. Our club has its arms open for everyone who shows interest in the arts, and not just architecture!
